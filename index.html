About

I'm a three-time Emmy Award-winning video editor with over 25 years of experience crafting compelling stories for children's television and beyond. My work focuses on creating content that educates, entertains, and inspires young audiences.

My extensive work on Sesame Street has allowed me to contribute to one of television's most iconic and impactful programs, helping shape how children learn and engage with media.

Beyond editing, I'm committed to nurturing the next generation of creative professionals through my role as a mentor in the Children's Media Association, and as founder of the Patricia A. LaPorte Scholarship for the Creative Arts.

A graduate of the GoToEditor course and certified in DaVinci Resolve's Editor & Fairlight pages, I maintain expertise across industry-standard platforms. At Sesame Street, I work principally in the Adobe suite, ensuring seamless workflow and exceptional results.
